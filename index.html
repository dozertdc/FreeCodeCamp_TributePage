Tommy Kono

Here's a timeline of Tommy Kono's life:

  • 1930 - Born in Sacramento, CA
  • 1942 - Relocated to Tule Lake internment camp during WWII
  • 1942 - Introduced to weightlifting at Tule Lake
  • 1946 - Released from internment camp
  • 1950 - Drafted into the U.S. Army
  • 1952 - Gold medalist at the Olympics
  • 1956 - Gold medalist at the Olympics
  • 1960 - Silver medalist at the Olympics
  • 1978 - Inducted into the Hawaii Sports Hall of Fame
  • 1993 - Elected into the IWF Hall of Fame
  • 2005 - IWF names Kono the "Lifter of the Century"
  • 2016 - dies at the age of 85.
